Join thousands of volunteers creating positive change right here at home. Find meaningful opportunities that fit your schedule and passion.

Current Volunteer Opportunities

Food Bank Assistant

Help sort and distribute food to families in need. Flexible 3-4 hour shifts on weekends. No experience required - training provided.

Youth Mentor

Guide local teenagers with homework and life skills. 2 hours per week commitment. Background in education helpful but not required.

Senior Companion

Provide friendship and assistance to elderly community members. Visit for 1-2 hours weekly. Great for compassionate individuals.

Community Garden Helper

Maintain local garden that provides fresh produce to food pantries. Saturday mornings, seasonal work. Perfect for nature lovers.

Animal Shelter Support

Care for rescue animals awaiting adoption. Various shifts available. Must be comfortable with cats and dogs.

Homeless Shelter Volunteer

Serve meals and provide support to individuals experiencing homelessness. Evening shifts, 4-6 hours monthly.

Getting Started FAQ

How much time do I need to commit?

Opportunities range from 2 hours monthly to several hours weekly. You choose what works for your schedule.

Do I need special skills or experience?

Most positions welcome beginners. We provide training and support to help you succeed.

Is there an age requirement?

Opportunities exist for volunteers aged 14 and up. Some positions may have specific age requirements.

What about background checks?

Positions working with children, seniors, or vulnerable populations require background checks, which we help coordinate.

What should I expect at my first volunteer session?

You'll receive orientation, meet your coordinator, and be paired with an experienced volunteer to show you the ropes.
